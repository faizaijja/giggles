Rwanda as Study Focus

Chosen due to growing emphasis on digital learning initiatives, emerging recognition of learning disabilities, and rapid technological advancements creating ideal environment for testing edutainment platforms.

Problem Statement

Core Problem

Children with learning disabilities in Rwanda are often left behind due to scarcity of inclusive educational resources and platforms.

Specific Challenges

  • Educational Exclusion: Traditional learning methods fail to accommodate unique learning needs
  • Academic Impact: Poor performance and low self-esteem
  • Social Isolation: Sense of exclusion from learning process
  • Resource Scarcity: Limited access to specialized learning tools

Proposed Solution

"Giggles" - A digital edutainment platform offering specialized, fun, and interactive learning experiences that:

  • Enhance cognitive abilities
  • Improve academic performance
  • Boost social confidence
  • Provide inclusive learning environment
